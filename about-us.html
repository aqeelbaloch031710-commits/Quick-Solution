About Us

At Quick Solution, we believe that every home and office deserves top-quality services without delays or hidden costs. That’s why we’ve built a platform that connects you with skilled professionals who are ready to solve your problems — fast and reliably.

From urgent repairs to planned upgrades, we cover a wide range of services: electrician, plumbing, painting, carpentry, duct & vent cleaning, handyman support, house shifting, water tank services, window & door installation, AC maintenance, and appliance repair.

Our leadership team, led by Aqeel Baloch (CEO) and Salman Ayub (Co-CEO), is committed to ensuring every client experiences convenience, trust, and professionalism at every step.

With Quick Solution, you can count on us to make your life easier — on time, every time.
